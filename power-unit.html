The new power units

The new 2026 F1 regulations introduce 50/50 split between combustion engine and electric power. The new electric power known as MGU-K has triple the power of its predecessor MGU-H.

The Internal Combustion Engine (ICE)

Since the foundation remains the same as last years, 1.6-liter turbocharged V6, the internal undergoes massive changes. Now running with 100% sustainable fuel the cars operate with a net-zero carbon footprint.

Motor Generator Unit - Kinetic (MGU-K)

Now that the cars are more environmentally friendly, changing to this fuel brings its own demerits such as loss in power. To counter that, MGU-K harvests energy during braking from the rear axle will generate almost 350kW (470hp). This is almost 3 times as much as power from last year's MGU-H.
